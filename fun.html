My Elite Story

I was born and raised in Queens (melting pot of the world!) and come from a mixed ethnic background. My brother and I were exposed to food from varied cultures (including our own). And unlike many children, we ate everything that was served to us and enjoyed everything we ate!

This only evolved in adulthood as we increased our circles of friends and eventually the word "Foodie" was used by many to describe me to others.

Some cringe at the term (not sure why?) while the rest of us consider it a like interest and use it as an excuse to bond. ("YOU'RE a Foodie too? We HAVE to hang out! I could use a new Food Buddy and I've been craving ramen lately.")

Eventually I discovered Yelp and decided to not only use it to look up restaurant reviews, but to put my experiences to good use by adding to the reviews and photos of restaurants that I, myself relied on from others.

At some point it came up that a couple of friends are Yelp Elite members, and one of them was honestly in total shock that I never heard of it. ("What do you mean YOU aren't ELITE? You get invited to all these food and drink tasting events for FREE!")

Other Yelp Events

There are also "Official Yelp Events" that are open to the everyone (not just Yelp Elite members). Some are free, some require a ticket purchase.

Sometimes other Yelp users submit events that are not sponsored by Yelp, so look for "Official Yelp Event" to be sure.

New York Techweek 2016 click the link to see the Yelp page

Techweek is an example of an event that is not an Official Yelp Event but is listed in the "Festivals & Fairs" category. It is a very popular event that draws a lot of interest, so Yelp promotes on it's events page and also offers a chance to win a ticket for free admission.

Techweek New York launched in 2013 with a small and intimate attendee list of 500, while this year we are anticipating thousands. And we take this mission very seriously. Techweek New York 2016 happens over a week, and it happens in multiple locations: highlighting some of the headquarters, in-office charms, and culture of New York's emerging technology greats; and some of its very much emerged technology businesses too.
